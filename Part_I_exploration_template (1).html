What is the structure of your dataset?¶

There are 133,937 rows of loan data and 81 columns of variables. Most variables are numeric in nature with a few variables like ProsperRating(Numeric), Prosper(Alpha) and ProsperScore being ordinal.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

My main interest is to determine the features that are responsible in predicting LoanStatus.Further,to specifically find out the features responsible in determining whether a Loan will be defaulted, completed or chargedoff.

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

My expectations are that IncomeVerifiable,ProsperScore,BorrowerState and the EmploymentStatus variables will be the determining variables in regards to predicting LoanStatus. However, i consider other variables to help support my investigation. The variables include the following:

  • ListingCategory (numeric)
  • Prosper Score
  • CreditScoreMean(To be derived from the average of CreditScoreRangeUpper and CreditScoreRangeLower)
  • EmploymentStatus
  • IsBorrowerHomeowner
  • Occupation
  • IncomeVerifiable
  • BorrowerState
  • MonthlyLoanPayment
  • Term
